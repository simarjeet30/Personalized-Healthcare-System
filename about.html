Welcome to your personalised healthcare system


This is a website for our Sem 6 minor project where we help users figure out if they might have a common disease based on the symptoms they are experiencing.
In the second section, we take in patient data and use a logistic regression model trained on previous patient data to predict whether the concerned patient is infected with COVID-19 or not.

The user has to login in order to use this service. They can also see all previous patient data (without their names for anonymity).

The user can also check how the current humidity and temperature conditions of their room favor the spread of COVID-19, so they can take appropriate precautions if they happen to be favorable to COVID-19 spread.
